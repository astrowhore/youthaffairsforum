    ~~~ What is the Youth Affairs Forum ~~~

    The Youth Affairs Forum (YAF) is an organization that empowers diverse youth by fostering global understanding and leadership, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to make a lasting impact on the world through conferences, competitions, internships, and blogs.
    We, as a student-led organization with the aim of spreading understanding and commitment to Political and International Affairs through Model United Nations, Debate Journeys etc, seek to facilitate the development of collaboration skills, help first-timers with their MUN and debate journeys, and through well-articulated blog posts and ensuing discussions, enhance understanding of political systems and processes.
